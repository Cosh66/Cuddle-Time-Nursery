What Makes Us Amazing!

Hello from Cuddle Time Nursery

We are thrilled to announce that Cuddle Time Nursery, a secure and caring setting for children ages two to four, will soon open. Our staff is committed to giving your child an enjoyable and instructive experience so they can develop and flourish, providing a clean, inviting atmosphere for your young ones. Constructed with contemporary amenities, it offers a secure and supportive environment for young children to develope, study, and have fun. We are thrilled to provide your children the ideal start to their early years with an experienced staff that places a strong emphasis on creativity and exploration!

About Our Nursery

We have a Spacious bungalow, with a large garden area for run around play time, we also have a park to swing like little monkey's, and a garden area to grow there own plants. Through a range of activities inside our nursery, children may explore, learn, and develop in a creative and safe environment at Cuddle Time Nursery. And our team is dedicated to giving your children the best care possible and has received extensive training in child development.
