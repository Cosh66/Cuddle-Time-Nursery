Keeping your children safe at Cuddle Time Nursery

Children's safety and wellbeing are our first priorities at Cuddle Time Nursery. To keep every child in our care secure, we adhere to stringent safeguarding procedures.

Our safeguarding procedures include:

  • (Disclosure and Barring Service) checks on every employee.
  • All employees should receive regular training on safeguarding.
  • A defined procedure for reporting worries regarding the wellbeing of a youngster.
  • collaborating closely with local government agencies to guarantee adherence to safeguarding laws.
  • Strict protocols for child protection during drop-off and pick-up times.

Please feel free to contact our designated safeguarding lead with any questions you may have about safeguarding link hear for more information safeguarding@cuddletimenursey.com.
